About Me

I'm the Senior Director of Digital Marketing at Heifer International — a global nonprofit fighting hunger and poverty — where I've spent over 20 years building digital programs, brand strategy, and content ecosystems that move people to act.

Alongside my full-time role, I run Rare Gem Media, a creative design and media company where I partner with brands and businesses on strategy, content, and digital presence.

I also write Southern Blondie — a personal blog that's a real, unfiltered reflection of my life. The good stuff, the messy stuff, and everything in between.

I'm also a mom, a lifestyle content creator, a golf dabbler, and a firm believer that busy women can build big things — without burning out.

Pro Bono Initiative

Little Rock:
You're Open.

I founded this program through Rare Gem Media to close the digital gap for Little Rock's local food businesses — the restaurants, caterers, and cottage kitchens that feed our community but often don't have a web presence to match.

Every month, one food business receives a professionally designed, fully built website — completely free. Because every business deserves to be found.

#LittleRockYoureOpen

One free website.
Every month.
For Little Rock.

A pro bono web presence initiative dedicated to local food businesses in Little Rock, AR — because showing up online shouldn't cost a community its flavor.
